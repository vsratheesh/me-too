ME TOO

Change starts with ideas. When ideas become reality, they transform lives. At ME TOO, we inspire, mentor, fund and incubate, early stage, innovation-based social enterprises that impact the lives of India's poor. We support you to make your idea a reality.

Mission

To be a credible organisation that upholds the values of accountability and transparency in every activity it undertakes.

WHO WE ARE

Social service organisations makes schemes that centers its aims at the welfare of the society and its individuals along with the progress of the nation. To achieve transparent and effiecient social service activities, there is a need for a service enterprise that has the most effective organizing structure and works through proffesional methods.

How you can help
